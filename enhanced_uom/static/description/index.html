Cross category UOM

use different ratios for same UOMs for different materials

You can mix units from different types in one category and let odoo do all required calculations. Below is an example of using different UOMs of metal plates in manufacturing:

  1. UOMs:
    • Purchase - Unit
    • Manufacutre - BOM a cm2
    • Or Manufacutre - BOM b mm2
    • Stock calculation - kg
  2. You need to define ratio to convert from kg to Unit, from kg to cm2 , and from kg to mm3
  3. You can buy plates of different sizes and weight - and the purchase uom can be set to Unit - Odoo will automatically convert stock to kg - You can use that material on BOM with f.e cm2 or mm3, you can define individual ratio per UOM
